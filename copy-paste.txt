1. local X linux buffer works when text highlighted in yellow with mouse drag or v ... y or CTL Shft C

2. above local copy can be pasted with p in NVIM or middle mouse click

3. System buffer(windows as well) copy to by text select by hold Shft key drag over text then CTL Shft C or Edit menu copy or rt click in Konsole

4. System & Win buffer paste by CTL Shft V or middle mouse click in linux
